Output

Please set your Current Level, Goal Level, and Current XP Towards Next Level, and then click CALCULATE to generate your results!

ABOUT

FFXIV PVP Malmstone XP Calculator tool created by belthesar, based off of Malmstone XP Calculator by NanaGrace.

This tool is designed to help you calculate how many malmstones you need to reach a given PVP Malmstone Series Level goal. Your current progress is saved in your browser's Local Storage, so you can come back and update your progress without having to re-enter everything.
